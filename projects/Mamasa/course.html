This course teaches students about the role of costume design in film production. Students will learn about the history and evolution of film costume design, as well as the creative and technical aspects of designing costumes for film characters. They will also learn about collaboration with other departments on set, such as the director, production designer, and makeup artist. Students will explore the different types of films, such as period films, fantasy, sci-fi, and contemporary films, and how costumes contribute to the storytelling of each. They will also learn about the process of creating and executing a costume design, including research, sketching, fabric selection, fittings, and final costume construction. Students will have opportunities to create their own costume designs for film characters and learn how to pitch their ideas to a production team.
