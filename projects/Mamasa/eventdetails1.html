TFW - SOUTH INDIAN DESIGNER CONTEST 2023

Uncage Your Creativity & Take home the title -

"South Indian Best Fashion Designer 2023"

Fashion Designing is one of the fastest-growing industries in India. There has always been a demand for good designers, but getting the required exposure for the same is difficult, especially for young designers. In such a way, TFW offers a unique opportunity to delve into the field of fashion.

TFW 2023 platform is exclusively for new designers from across South India, inspired by different fashion themes , and they design to attract, entertain, and educate the audience on fashion styles and trends.

What's More , this grand event will feature a showcase in front of industrial experts, fashion icons, and film personalities and massive audience, providing an unparalleled opportunity for participants to make their mark on the fashion world.From press social media / coverage to magazine features, the exquisite fashion week will be showcased in all its glory, leaving audiences astonished and mesmerized.

If you are a new designer applying to be a part of this program, you must have a background in fashion as an emerging designer, freelancer, or fashion student. You should not be under the professional designer label or with a commercial brand.

"All designer applicants must present their credentials and designs for Men's and Women's wear in equal numbers, in accordance with the given theme, to qualify for the COLLECTION SHOWCASE."

"For the SOLO DESIGN SHOWCASE, you have the freedom to choose the gender and theme of the garment."
