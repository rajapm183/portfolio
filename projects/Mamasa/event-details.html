1.Fundamentals of Fashion Design course:
In this comprehensive course, students will learn the essential principles and techniques that form the foundation of successful fashion design. Explore fashion history, elements of design, color theory, and textile knowledge. Develop skills in sketching fashion illustrations, creating mood boards, and conceptualizing design ideas. Gain hands-on experience in garment construction, including pattern making and sewing techniques. Dive into fabric selection, draping, and understanding body proportions. This course nurtures creativity and prepares students for a dynamic career in the fashion industry.
