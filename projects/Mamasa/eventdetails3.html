EVENT DETAILS
TFW -MULTIPLE FASHION CONTESTS

"Unleash the Artisan in You "
"Introducing Bold New Contests for fashion enthusiasts! If you have an innovative and creative mind, and have worked (or wish to work) on fashion kicks, this competition is for you! Show the magic of your hand and let your inner artist shine! This is your chance to showcase the artisan in you and take your fashion career to the next level. Don't miss the opportunity to showcase your talent and win exciting prizes."

Contests Are....

-Window Display
-Fashion illustration
-Style War
-Fashion Face-off
-Dress it up

1. FASHION ILLUSTRATION:

Brainstorm your dreamy ideas of fashion on paper that are visually appealing.

RULES:
- Three themes will be given on the spot 30 minutes prior to the event commencing.
- Sketch sheets will be provided.
- Contestants must bring their own sketch kits.

2. STYLE WAR:

Be stylish and perfect from head to toe, and grab the title - STYLE ICON TFW 2023!

RULES:
- Style yourself with your signature style.
- Influence it!
- Give us your own style statement.

3. DRESS IT UP:

Wrap up and style a doll with folds of cloth or pieces of paper to display your fashion skills.

RULES:
- Theme will be given on the spot prior to the event commencing.
- A Miniature Doll should be brought by the Participant (Plain Barbie Doll M or F).
- Contestants must bring their own styling kits and aesthetic items.

4. WINDOW DISPLAY:

Make them stop, look back, and walk to your store front - create a fashion window display miniature.

RULES:
- Miniature size is a 2/2 feet cube.
- Miniature must be done and brought to the spot.
- The theme of the visual merchandising can be of your choice but within fashion and fashion only.
- On the spot explanation about your creation within the set time limit.

5. FACE OFF:

Bored painting on a surface? Why not on a face?

RULES:
- On-spot theme based on fashion.
- Required materials must be bought by the participant.
- The participant can either work on their own face or bring a model.

For Winners -
- Awards + Certificates + Monetary Price.
