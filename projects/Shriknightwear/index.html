Our Company

Shri Knitwear is a reputable and fast growing knitwear company based in Tirupur, India. Shri Knitwear was established in the year 2010 with a dedicated team of highly experienced and professional people from the knitwear manufacturing industry. We engage in manufacturing and exporting knitted garments for various retail brands and chain stores across the globe, We offer our clients with products of impeccable quality, on-time delivery, competitive price, reliable, committed service & more

"Our aim is to supply quality fashion products at a competitive price to brands across the globe”

Our Product

Shri knitwear supplies a wide range of knit garments in various style, design and colors.

We have a range of quality products for Men’s, Women’s, Childrens and Infants from "Basics to High Fashion" We work with all kinds of knit fabric materials such as 100% Cotton, Cotton/Elastane, Polyester, Cotton/Polyester, Viscose, Modal, Organic Cotton, 100% Polyester and others

Our Service

We monitor our entire supply chain from the stage of product development till the final goods are delivered. We work based on our client requirements and start our sample development process based on their design details.-"Sampling to Shipment"

Sampling / R & D

We have a skilled sampling team for developing new sample collection with less turn around time, we continously work on our research & development in all the areas of knitwear industry focusing in developing special yarns, new fabrics, prints, washes finishing techniques & more

Flexible M.O.Q

We work with brands and chain stores across Europe, U.K, Australia and New Zealand, We are also flexible in working with lower order quantities for aspiring start up fashion brands across the world, our quality standards are consistently maintained in our product and in our supply chain for all our orders.

Vertically Integrated

Our supply chain is completely integrated with the capacity to produce high volume of goods with fine workmanship within a very short leadtime. Our production facility is continously updated with new technologies in process and machineries to produce quality and value added products.
