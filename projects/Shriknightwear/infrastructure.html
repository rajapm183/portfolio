Sampling

We have skilled sampling team for developing new sample collection based on our buyer’s design and technical styling, We present regular sample’s in new fabrics, prints and wash collections to our buyer’s so that they can have various options to try them.

Production

Our supply chain is completely integrated with the capacity to produce high volume of goods with fine workmanship in a very short lead time. Our products are inspected and tested based on international standards. Our production facility is continuously updated with new technology, process and machineries to produce quality and value added products.

R & D

We continuously work on R & D in all the areas of the knitwear manufacturing sector focusing in developing new fabrics with different knit structures and fiber content, we under take developing new printing and washing techniques for our buyers.
